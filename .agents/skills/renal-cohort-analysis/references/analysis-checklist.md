# Renal Cohort Analysis Checklist

Check and record:

- cohort inclusion, exclusion, and index date;
- exposure, comparator, outcome, and follow-up definitions;
- baseline kidney status, comorbidities, concurrent medications, and care-intensity differences;
- missingness, censoring, and selection bias;
- subgroup definition and sample sufficiency;
- whether the output is descriptive, associative, or supported by a stronger causal design.

Use cautious language: `observed association`, `signal`, `hypothesis`, and `requires validation`.
