# Evidence Decision Pack

Produce the artifact a scientist or research governance group can review.

## Workflow

1. State the candidate-target-subgroup hypothesis and decision question.
2. Link every supporting claim to a source, data type, and limitation.
3. Separate structural plausibility, trial evidence, and care-delivery evidence.
4. Propose `GO`, `HOLD`, or `NO-GO` as a recommendation for human review, not an autonomous decision.
5. Specify the smallest next study or evidence request that would reduce the largest uncertainty.

## Output

Return a decision pack with: hypothesis, evidence summary, provenance, limitations, proposed decision, required reviewer, and next-study options. Use [the decision-pack schema](references/decision-pack-schema.md).

## Boundary

Label all synthetic outputs as synthetic. Preserve negative evidence. Do not turn a score into a treatment recommendation.
